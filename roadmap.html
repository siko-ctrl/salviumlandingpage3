Our Roadmap

Discover the journey ahead as we revolutionize private blockchain technology with compliance and innovation at its core.

01

Phase 1: Launch Salvium

Staking and Yield Generation

Users can stake their SAL tokens to earn rewards, fostering a secure and engaged network through Salvium's evolved privacy capabilities.

Compliance

Introduction of refundable transactions, allowing recipients to return unauthorized funds to senders without address requests - a groundbreaking feature for Monero-based chains.

02

Phase 2: Additional Compliance Features

Private Blockchain with Compliance Features

Meeting EU's MiCA regulations while maintaining transaction privacy through authorized view key access for exchanges and regulatory entities.

Technology Advancement

Supporting and contributing to compliance efforts including Full chain membership proofs, SERAPHIS, and JAMTIS, furthering Monero and Cryptonote technologies.

03

Phase 3: DeFi Support

Smart Contract Functionality

Enabling secure and private DeFi applications through protocol_tx innovation and smart contract capabilities.

Middleware for Solidity

Streamlining adoption by providing middleware for easy deployment of Solidity-based DApps on the Salvium network.
